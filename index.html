Surface Options

Three Premium Finishes

Every finish is fully integrated during the molding process — ensuring durability, moisture resistance, and lasting brand integrity.

Matte

A smooth, non-reflective surface conveying sophistication and modern restraint. Reduces glare under retail lighting with a soft-touch visual appeal. Ideal for premium food, dairy, and clean-label brands.

Refined · Anti-Glare

Glossy

A vibrant, high-shine surface that enhances color depth and sharp graphic detail. Maximizes visual impact on shelf — perfect for bold branding, high-contrast designs, and strong visual appeal.

High-Glaze · Vivid Color

Foil / Metallic

A striking, reflective surface delivering premium, upscale shelf presence. Creates eye-catching metallic accents in gold, silver, or custom tones. Ideal for specialty, limited-edition, or luxury packaging.

Gold · Silver · Custom

How It Works

From artwork to shelf.

A streamlined four-step process from initial brief to delivered product.

Step 01

Brief & Spec

Share your volume, format needs, and brand artwork. We confirm specifications and select the right container from our catalog.

Step 02

Artwork Approval

Our team prepares print-ready IML film artwork and sends proofs for your review. Finish selection (matte, glossy, foil) is confirmed at this stage.

Step 03

Molding & Integration

IML film is placed in the mold before injection. The label fuses permanently to the container — zero post-processing required.

Step 04

Pack & Ship

Cases are packed to spec and shipped direct. Standard case counts range from 500 to 2,000 pieces depending on format.
